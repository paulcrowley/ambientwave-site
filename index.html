Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

AmbientWave is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's what you need to know:

  • No data collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • No analytics: We do not track your usage or behavior within the app.
  • No account required: Use the app without signing up or providing any information.
  • Offline functionality: All sounds are stored locally on your device. No internet connection is required.
  • No third-party services: We do not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising services.
